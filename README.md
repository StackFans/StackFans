## 🚀 About StackFans

StackFans is a blockchain-powered creator platform built on Stack, enabling creators to monetize exclusive content while maintaining full revenue ownership, data sovereignty, and enterprise-grade security compliance.

We combine wallet-based authentication, end-to-end encryption (AES-256-GCM), and decentralized infrastructure to deliver a privacy-first alternative to traditional subscription platforms.

---

## 🔐 Core Principles

- **100% Creator Revenue** — No platform cuts, only minimal gas fees  
- **Privacy by Design** — Pseudonymous access, no unnecessary personal data  
- **End-to-End Encryption** — AES-256-GCM with per-content key isolation  
- **Wallet-Native Authentication** — Cryptographic signature verification  
- **Real-Time Payments** — Instant on-chain transactions  
- **Compliance-Ready** — Designed around NIST 800-53 & ISO 27001 controls  

---

## 🌐 Vision

To empower creators with full economic freedom, uncompromising privacy, and cryptographically verifiable ownership — without sacrificing usability or compliance.
